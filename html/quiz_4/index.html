Spaghetti

Definition

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical, solid pasta. It is staple food of traditional italian cuisine like other pasta, spaghetti is made of milled wheat and water. Italian spaghetti is made from durum wheat semolina, but elsewhere it may be made with other kinds of flour.

Recipe

  • Dried spaghetti
  • Red sauce (vodka sauce, marinara)
  • Grated permesan
  • Butter or Olive oil
  • Seasoning (garlic,salt, pepper, oregano)

Preparation

  • Bring a pot of boiling water.
  • Add spaghetti.
  • Allow to return to boil.
  • Boil the spaghetti for 7-15min.
  • Pour the paste through a strainer.

Conclusion

  • Heat some sauce, and pour it over the spaghetti.
  • Top it off with melted butter and grated parmeson cheese, basil or oregano, If desired.
