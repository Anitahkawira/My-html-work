About Us

Cubits is a Team of innovative and committed Students from AkiraChix, Who are passionate about tech innovations and are coming up with a pre-paid water meter that enables users to get water as per the amount.


Our Product offers unmeasurable value to our users, both water consumers and Administator. For Administrator, No unpaid water bills since your user can have the plivilenge of seeing how their are consuming water. Since alot of cash is spend on printing the bills and going to the stations to pay, Our product comes hand in hand and save you and timer and resources.


For our Consumer we offer them with the power to control they expenditure. We offer Convient in terms of money and time. And the all process is very easy to use since we are always an sms away
